More About Me

I began my professional journey in Arequipa, Peru, earning both my Bachelor's degree and Professional License in Industrial Engineering. This foundation allowed me to develop strong skills in Process Optimization and Project Management across diverse industries, including Transportation, Retail, Logistics, Healthcare, and Services. After three years of hands-on experience, I sought to broaden my skill set in the U.S. While pursuing my MBA, I discovered a deep interest in data analysis through webinars and industry events. I realized the significant role that data plays in driving business decisions in the U.S., far more prominently than in my home country. This realization led me to pursue a Master's in Business Analytics in New York, where I graduated with honors, maintaining a 4.0 GPA. Since then, I have honed my technical skills in SQL, Python, and R, working on various projects to apply my knowledge in real-world scenarios. I'm continually updating my expertise, with a focus on emerging technologies such as Machine Learning and AI. I am now actively seeking opportunities to leverage my skills and apply my expertise in data analysis to drive business outcomes, as showcased in my portfolio.
